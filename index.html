I am a postdoc at the University of Copenhagen supported by a Walter Benjamin Fellowship of the DFG. My main research area is number theory and my interests include algebraic geometry, automorphic forms, and high energy physics.

Here you can find my CV, my thesis, the slides of my defense, and a case study introducing my field of research to non-specialists.
